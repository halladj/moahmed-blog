## Who I Am

I am **Dr. Mohamed**, a licensed clinical pharmacist with a passion for improving patient outcomes through evidence-based pharmaceutical care. With years of experience in both community and hospital pharmacy settings, I bring a unique perspective to medication management and patient education.

## Education

- **Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD)** — University of Pharmacy
- **Bachelor of Science in Pharmaceutical Sciences** — University of Science
- **Board Certified Pharmacotherapy Specialist (BCPS)**

## Areas of Expertise

- **Clinical Pharmacy** — Patient-centered medication therapy management
- **Medication Safety** — Reducing medication errors and adverse drug events
- **Pharmaceutical Research** — Contributing to evidence-based pharmacy practice
- **Patient Education** — Empowering patients to understand their medications
- **Drug Information** — Providing accurate, up-to-date drug information to healthcare professionals

## Professional Experience

Over the course of my career, I have worked across multiple pharmacy settings including:

- Hospital and clinical pharmacy
- Community retail pharmacy
- Pharmaceutical consulting
- Academic teaching and mentorship

## My Mission

I believe that pharmacists play a critical role in the healthcare team. My mission is to bridge the gap between complex pharmaceutical science and practical patient care. Through this website, I share my knowledge, experiences, and insights to help both healthcare professionals and patients make informed decisions about medications.
